How to Navigate to someone elses server?
* look for foreignObject element, the first one is discord logo, second is your own server 
  and from index 2 (3rd element) , the 3rd party servers start.
  
* To scrap a specific server I will need to find out the index of servers logo in left
  menu of discord.

* To get a certain channel in server find elements with class "containerDefault-3TQ5YN"
